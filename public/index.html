I Am A Front-End Developer Who Is Looking To Grow Into Something More

If I have learned anything over the last five years since I started getting into web development, is that I absolutely love what I do, and am a slave to quality.

Since 2014, I have worked as a web developer primarily focused on JavaScript and Ruby / Ruby-on-Rails based applications, with a pivot to PHP and Wordpress over the last two years. My experience ranges from small early stage startups to large enterprise level companies. Over this time I have been responsible for developing a range of different products including custom content management / live-service applications, brochure and multi-site applications, and been the frontrunner in various efforts towards making projects I am involved with as accessible as possible given time constraints. I have been responsible for the full life-cycle of development including initial requirement gathering, code design, development, testing, continuous integration and deployment, and debugging.

I am confident my experience and strive to stay adept in the practice of front-end development as my skill-sets advance, though I am always eager to expand my horizons into becoming something more. I look forward to a chance to discuss my background further and to meet you and your team for future collaboration.
